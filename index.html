CaptureCode – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

CaptureCode does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers owned by the developer.

Data stored locally

  • Your Gemini API key (stored only in your browser via chrome.storage.local)
  • Your preferred output language
  • Your daily usage count

This data never leaves your device and is never shared with anyone.

Third-party services

When you use CaptureCode, a screenshot of the selected area is sent to the Google Gemini API for code generation. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Pro subscriptions are managed by ExtensionPay. Payment data is handled by Stripe. See ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy.
